# Keyset 1.1.2

Adds NeoForge support for **Minecraft 26.1** — completing 26.1 loader coverage alongside the Fabric/Quilt jar from `1.1.1`.

## What's new

- NeoForge support for Minecraft 26.1, with full feature parity: profiles, conflict browser, Safe Fix, clipboard JSON, and the Controls-screen button on both the `Controls` page and the `Key Binds` sub-screen.

## Support policy

- `1.1.x` targets Minecraft `1.20.1+` and `26.1` on Fabric, Quilt, and NeoForge.
- Forge 26.1 support is pending upstream loader tooling.
- Older `1.16.5–1.19.4` builds stay on the `1.0.x` line for critical bug fixes only.

# Keyset 1.1.1

Adds Fabric and Quilt support for **Minecraft 26.1** — the first fully unobfuscated Minecraft release.

## What's new

- Fabric and Quilt support for Minecraft 26.1, with full feature parity: profiles, conflict browser, Safe Fix, clipboard JSON, and the Controls-screen button.
- The Keyset button now appears on both the `Controls` page and the `Key Binds` sub-screen (MC 26.1 splits these into two separate screens).

## Support policy

- `1.1.x` targets Minecraft `1.20.1+` and now `26.1` on Fabric/Quilt.
- Forge and NeoForge 26.1 support is pending upstream loader tooling.
- Older `1.16.5–1.19.4` builds stay on the `1.0.x` line for critical bug fixes only.

Keyset `1.3.0` adds a much faster way to swap setups in-game: you can now bind `Activate Profile Slot 1-5` and jump straight to your first five saved profiles without opening the menu first.

This update grew out of [issue #2](https://github.com/BeeBoyD/Keyset/issues/2), and thanks go to `PhunghisKhan` for the suggestion that pushed this feature onto the roadmap.

It also cleans up the Controls screen a bit by moving Keyset binds into their own `Keyset` category, so the open-screen, cycle, and slot shortcuts are all grouped together instead of being buried in misc.

No migration is needed for existing configs. Your current profile order is still the source of truth, and the new slot binds just follow that order.
